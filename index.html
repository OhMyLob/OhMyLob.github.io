AnyPark

AnyPark is the first social network where you can share parkings! Launch the app, enable GPS and register your car to share your parking with the community in just a few taps: users will see a mark on the map with the relative information, fully respectful of your privacy! AnyPark is not just about saving time and fuel: every time you help a user finding a spot you receive credits which give you access to many discounts for your car!
Emoticon Picker

Emoticon Picker is a free and open source app that sends emoticons from your Android phone or Android Wear device directly to your PC wirelessly. First, you open the PC program, then you open the app on the phone; the setup is very simple and automatic.
Umbrella Alert

Umbrella Alert is a simple and elegant open source weather app that notifies you if it's going to rain. You can select where and when the app should check the weather. It's possible to enable WiFi before the app checks if it'll rain. If it's not going to rain, the app won't send any notification.
Paper Launcher

This is an Android Launcher app made from scratch. The launcher doesn't include much customization, you can only change wallpaper, the dock icons and the Icon Pack, as it's only been an experiment to me, trying to do my best with smooth animation and transitions, bringing a new modern look to my phone. The second dock that appears when you slide on the first dock or open the App drawer is dynamic and brings your most launched apps always visible. The app includes Homescreen gestures, swipe up to open the App drawer, swipe down to expand the StatusBar. Android Nougat 7.1 Shortcuts are supported with a new layout that uses the Material BottomSheet component.
